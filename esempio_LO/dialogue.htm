A BUSY DINNER

read  the dialogue  

 

Story Summary

 

A man enters a busy diner, finds a table and sits down. The waitress is overworked and he tries to attract her attention to ask for the menu. The waitress takes a long time to come and, he becomes increasingly irritated. He is finally given the menu and his order is taken. His meal is served and to his dismay it tastes awful! When the bill arrives he is shocked at how expensive it is. He goes out after leaving a very small tip.

 

 

 

Customer: C                                   

Waitress : W

C : Er… excuse me! Could I have a menu, please?

few minutes pass……….

C: Can I have the menu please?

C: The menu?               

                                      

After a few minutes……….   

W : Here you are sir. What would you like?

C :  I'm not quite ready yet.

W : OK. I'll come back when you are.

C : No … wait a minute. I think I'll have tomato soup and chicken and chips.

W: One soup and one chicken and chips.

C : No, I've changed my mind. I think I'd like vegetable soup and steak and chips.

W : Right. Vegetable soup and steak and chips.

C :  Wait a minute! I haven't finished yet. I'll have some tomatoes as well.

W : Right. I'll just take the menu.

C:  Just a minute. What kind of pie have you got?

W : Er… apple, blackberry, plum, raspberry and apricot.

C  : I'll have apple.

W : One apple-pie

A Few minutes later……

: Here you are.

C:  Thank you.

The customer starts eating and becomes very unhappy, he calls the waitress……

C  : This soup is tasteless and this is the toughest steak I've ever had!! And where is the apple in

        this apple pie?

W : I'm sorry- but it's all we've got.

The customer finishes his meal and signals for the bill….

W: Here you are, your bill sir.

The customer leaves leaving a small tip on the table.
